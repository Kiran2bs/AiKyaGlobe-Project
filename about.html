Our Craft

Channapatna lac-turnery, explained simply

Soft ivory wood is shaped on a hand or foot-powered lathe. While the wood spins, artisans press natural, vegetable-based lacquer sticks against it — the friction melts the lacquer onto the wood, building up the smooth, glossy colour bands the craft is famous for.
