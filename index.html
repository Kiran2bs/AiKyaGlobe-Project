Our Story

A craft older than the factories that tried to replace it

For over 200 years, artisans in Channapatna, Karnataka, have turned soft ivory wood on a hand lathe and finished it with natural, vegetable-dye lacquer. Every piece is made by these same artisan families — we simply help their work reach shelves overseas.
